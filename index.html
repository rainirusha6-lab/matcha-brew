Our Fusion Story

At Matcha Bistro, we believe comfort food shouldn't compromise on quality. Our barista bar features premium, ceremonial-grade organic Japanese Uji matcha mixed with bold dark espresso. Alongside our drinks, our chefs knead fresh sourdough daily to launch authentic blistering woodfired pizzas and stack prime Angus beef burgers.
